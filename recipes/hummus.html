Hummus

Description

Hummus is a middle eastern condiment made with chickpeas and tahini.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Baking Soda
  • Lemon Juice
  • Garlic
  • Sea Salt
  • Tahini
  • Ice Water
  • Ground Cumin
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Boil chickpeas with boiling soda and strain
  2. Blend with lemon juice, garlic and salt
  3. Add tahini and mix
  4. Drizzle in ice water, and add cumin
  5. Add cumin, then add olive oil
